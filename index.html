Getting started is easy

What is required?

To be a member of Verified by Freelancer, you need to have the following completed before applying:

  • Filled in your profile including uploading a recent photo
  • Phone verification complete
  • Identity verification (Know Your Customer) complete

How long does it take?

The process is usually complete within 24 hours.

What is the process?

It’s easy. Once you submit your application to get Verified, one of our agents will reach out to you to schedule a call. We will confirm your verification documents and identity, go through your profile in depth to ensure it is of the highest quality, and give you the blue Verified badge.
